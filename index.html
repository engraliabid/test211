About Our School

GBHS Hamid Ullah Bhatti is more than just a school; it's a community dedicated to academic rigor and personal growth. Situated in the tranquil setting of Basti Hamid Ullah Bhatti near Sadiqabad, we offer an environment where students can focus, learn, and thrive.

With an enrollment of exactly 426 bright students, our school maintains a vibrant educational atmosphere. We focus on academic excellence, moral values, and extracurricular development, ensuring every student is prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.
